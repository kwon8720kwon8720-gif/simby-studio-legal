Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Simby Studio handles information when you use our local publishing tools and this website.

1. Information we process

  • OAuth tokens: access/refresh tokens for YouTube and/or TikTok, stored locally on your device in a protected secrets file so the tools can upload on your behalf.
  • Video files and metadata: titles, captions, privacy settings, and media you choose to process/upload, handled locally and sent to the platform APIs you authorize.
  • Technical logs: local pipeline logs for debugging uploads (kept on your machine).

2. What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not run ads on this legal site.
  • We do not collect browsing analytics on these pages beyond standard GitHub Pages hosting logs controlled by GitHub.

3. Third-party platforms

When you connect YouTube or TikTok, those platforms receive data according to their own privacy policies. Review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • TikTok Privacy Policy

4. Storage and security

Credentials are intended to stay on your local machine with restricted file permissions. You are responsible for securing your device and revoking OAuth access when needed.

5. Data retention

Local tokens and logs remain until you delete them. Platform-side retention follows each platform’s policies after upload.

6. Children’s privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region).

7. Your choices

  • Revoke YouTube/TikTok access in each platform’s account security settings.
  • Delete local secret files and pipeline folders on your computer.

8. Changes

We may update this Policy. The effective date above will change when we do.

9. Contact

Privacy questions: contact the Simby Studio operator via the connected project channels.
